A B2B vehicle provenance and compliance platform for UK automotive dealers. Connects to 44 manufacturer databases including BMW, Audi, and Mercedes-Benz to pull official service history, flag mileage inconsistencies, and run HPI and finance checks through Experian and DVLA. Ships with PDI reports, Distance Sale Pack builder, automated appraisal, and an AI dispute response generator compliant with the Consumer Rights Act 2015 and Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013.
