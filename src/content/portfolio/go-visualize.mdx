<ProjectSection title="The Mission" number="01">

Marketing teams waste hours dragging pixels around Canva. Every social post, every ad, every flyer — same templating grind, different day. And the moment you need something that matches your actual brand guidelines, you're back to hunting down the right hex codes and font files.

**Go Visualize kills that workflow.** Type what you want in plain English. The AI generates production-ready, fully editable vector designs — brand colors, typography, layouts, everything. You tweak if you want. You ship. That's it.

</ProjectSection>

<ProjectSection title="Multi-Artboard Canvas Engine" number="02">

I built a **Figma-like vector editor** on top of Fabric.js — multiple artboards in a single workspace, real-time manipulation of text layers, high-res graphics, shapes, and grouped elements. The canvas state architecture handles simultaneous rendering across artboards without performance degradation, even with complex layered compositions.

</ProjectSection>

<ProjectSection title="Multi-Agent AI Design Orchestration" number="03">

This is where it gets interesting. I integrated a **dual-agent AI system** — Gemini 3 Flash Preview and Claude — that takes natural language prompts and converts them directly into structured, editable vector canvas designs.

The key insight? **Parallelization.** Both agents fire simultaneously on different aspects of the generation — one handles layout structure, the other handles visual styling. The results merge into a single coherent design. Parallelized execution inside Next.js backend means generation completes in seconds, not minutes.

</ProjectSection>

<ProjectSection title="Brand Guideline Extraction Engine" number="04">

Hands down the most technically fun subsystem. Drop in a company URL, and the platform **extracts the entire brand identity** — exact colour swatches, typographic hierarchy, logo positioning, visual design language — from scraping and analysing the public website. All of it gets loaded directly into the canvas editor configuration. No manual setup, no brand kit uploads. Just a URL.

</ProjectSection>

<ProjectSection title="Auth, Billing & Credit Ledger" number="05">

Authentication through **Better Auth** with secure session persistence. Billing is a three-tier architecture — Usage-Based, Monthly, and Annual — all wired through Stripe's modern Clover API. The twist? A **credit ledger system** that lets users purchase ad-hoc token packs to run heavy AI generation steps, separate from their subscription. Granular, elastic, self-serve.

</ProjectSection>

<ProjectSection title="Engineering Impact" number="06">

As **Technical Lead**, I set the architectural patterns — Prisma schema definitions, component paradigms, repository structure — and built the entire core system from February through early April 2026. When the platform was stable and optimised, I handed off a production-ready codebase to the internal team. Shipped a complex multi-agent AI product in under two months.

</ProjectSection>
